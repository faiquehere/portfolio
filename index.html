About Me

My journey with computers began way back with Windows XP, sparking a lifelong interest that turned into my career. I grew into an IT professional, skilled in web systems, online search strategies (SEO), and general IT work. Now, I'm taking that knowledge into cybersecurity, currently working on my Master's degree. I make sure to connect what I learn in school with real-world practice.

As a Cybersecurity Analyst, I focus on protecting digital systems. My hands-on experience in labs like TryHackMe and Hack The Box has taught me to keep networks safe, find weaknesses, perform ethical hacking, and respond quickly to security problems. I understand how to spot threats, defend against attacks, and make sure systems are secure from the ground up.

As a Digital Strategist, I use my tech skills to help online presences grow and succeed. I understand how to boost visibility through SEO, manage web systems, and create effective digital plans. My goal is to combine this ability to grow online reach with strong security, making sure that digital efforts are not just successful but also safe from cyber threats. I am passionate about building secure, flexible, and new solutions that protect our connected world.
